About the Festival

A one-of-a-kind experience.

Piroake Fest is our flagship festival; a celebration of music, community, and culture featuring live performances, interactive experiences, and unforgettable moments.

Karaoke Arena

Sing your heart out in our state-of-the-art karaoke zone, complete with professional sound and lighting.

Games Arena

Interactive games, competitions, and activities for everyone, from casual players to serious competitors.

Live Bands

Curated live performances across multiple genres, showcasing the best local and regional talent.
