About Us

Who We Are

Walking-Fish Group is a creative entertainment and experiences company focused on building culture-driven events, media, and unforgettable audience experiences.

The Name

Why “Walking-Fish”?

The name represents adaptability; a creature equally at home in two worlds, much like we operate across live experiences and media production. We move fluidly between bringing people together in physical spaces and capturing those moments for the digital world.

The Vision

Building an ecosystem.

To become one of Africa’s most innovative entertainment and experiences ecosystems where live events, media production, and community converge to create something bigger than any single experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Walking-Fish Group?

Walking-Fish Group (WFG) is the premier creative experiences, entertainment ecosystem, and high-end media production agency based in West Africa, with a particular operational focus in The Gambia. By bridging the physical gap between large-scale cultural events and sophisticated digital media, we create concepts that resonate deeply with audiences.

What kind of events does Walkie-Talkie Experiences organize?

Through our subsidiary Walkie-Talkie Experiences, we organize massive music festivals, beach raves, community picnics, and landmark creative events like Piroake Fest. Our events are designed to bring together thousands of people for unforgettable cultural and social interactions.

What services does Muster Point Pictures provide?

Through Muster Point Pictures, we produce professional commercial video, documentary features, brand storytelling campaigns, and cinematic drone footage for domestic and international brands. We capture the energy of live events and translate it into high-quality digital media.

Where does the name Walking-Fish come from?

The unique name "Walking-Fish" pays homage to the adaptability and resilience of the lungfish, an exceptional creature that thrives across multiple environments, transitioning effortlessly between water and dry land. This perfectly captures our dual nature: we are equally adept at orchestrating real-world events and developing high-quality digital media.

What is the mission of Walking-Fish Group?

At Walking-Fish Group, we are building an integrated network of events, technology, and media production that drives the cultural dialogue and provides significant value to our corporate sponsors, food and beverage vendors, creative partners, and audience members across West Africa.

Who We Are

Walking-Fish Group is a creative entertainment and experiences company focused on building culture-driven events, media, and unforgettable audience experiences. We are the parent company behind Walkie-Talkie Experiences (our live events and festivals arm) and Muster Point Pictures (our media and production arm).

We build unforgettable moments through live events, storytelling, production, and immersive experiences that bring people together. Our vision is to become one of Africa's most innovative entertainment and experiences ecosystems: not just event organisers, but a premium culture-driven company that shapes how people connect, celebrate, and remember.

Why the name Walking-Fish?

The name pays homage to the adaptability of the lungfish, a creature that thrives in both water and land. Like the lungfish, we operate across two distinct worlds: the physical world of live events and festivals, and the digital world of media, production, and storytelling. Adaptability is not just our philosophy; it is built into our identity.
