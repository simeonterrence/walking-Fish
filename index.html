More about Walking-Fish Group

Walking-Fish Group is a creative entertainment and experiences company based in The Gambia, West Africa, specializing in culture-driven festivals, live music events, karaoke experiences, picnic gatherings, rave parties, media production, drone cinematography, brand storytelling, and community-focused event design. Through our two subsidiaries, Walkie-Talkie Experiences and Muster Point Pictures, we produce flagship festivals like Piroake Fest, which combines karaoke arenas, live band performances, interactive games zones, VIP lounges, curated picnic areas, and vendor activations into a single immersive experience.

Our name represents adaptability, a creature equally at home in two worlds, much like we operate across live experiences and media production. We move fluidly between bringing people together in physical spaces and capturing those moments for the digital world. Our vision is to become one of Africa's most innovative entertainment and experiences ecosystems where live events, media production, and community converge.

Vendor opportunities at Walking-Fish Group events and festivals: we work with food and beverage vendors, merchandise sellers, games and activities operators, and sponsors. Benefits of becoming a vendor include access to thousands of festival attendees, prime booth placement options, marketing and promotion across our channels, dedicated vendor support team, and post-event sales data.

Contact us: General inquiries at hello@walkingfish.gm, vendor applications at vendors@walkingfish.gm, media and press at press@walkingfish.gm, and production inquiries at studio@walkingfish.gm.
