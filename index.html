Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does Walking-Fish Group do?

We are a creative entertainment and experiences company based in The Gambia. We specialize in culture-driven festivals, live music events, interactive karaoke nights, curated picnic gatherings, media production, and cinematic drone storytelling. Through our two subsidiaries, Walkie-Talkie Experiences and Muster Point Pictures, we bridge the gap between physical gatherings and digital media.

Why should I attend a Walkie-Talkie Experience event?

Our flagship events, like Piroake Fest, are designed to turn ordinary nights into unforgettable memories. We don't just host parties; we build immersive environments. You can expect high-energy karaoke arenas, premium live band performances, engaging interactive games zones, exclusive VIP lounges, and curated vendor activations all in one seamlessly designed place.

How can my business become a vendor or partner?

We regularly partner with food and beverage operators, merchandise creators, games operators, and corporate sponsors. By becoming a vendor at a Walking-Fish Group event, you gain direct access to thousands of engaged attendees. We offer prime booth placements, dedicated marketing promotion across our channels, comprehensive vendor support, and post-event insights. You can apply directly through our online Vendor Portal.

Where are you located and how do I contact you?

We operate primarily in The Gambia, West Africa, but our media and entertainment footprint is expanding across the continent. For general inquiries, you can reach us at hello@walkingfish.gm. If you are interested in vendor applications, email vendors@walkingfish.gm. For media and press coverage, contact press@walkingfish.gm, and for production inquiries, reach out to studio@walkingfish.gm.

What is the meaning behind the name Walking-Fish?

Our name represents ultimate adaptability—a creature equally at home in two entirely different worlds. Much like the walking fish, we operate fluidly across live physical experiences and high-quality digital media production. We seamlessly move between bringing people together in physical spaces and capturing those profound moments for the digital world, aiming to become Africa's most innovative entertainment ecosystem.
