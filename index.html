About Walking-Fish Group: Building Modern Cultural Ecosystems

Walking-Fish Group is a premier, culture-driven entertainment and experiences company dedicated to designing unforgettable physical and digital moments. Headquartered in West Africa, we operate as a parent ecosystem behind some of the region's most innovative entertainment sub-brands, specifically Walkie-Talkie Experiences (our specialized live events, picnics, and festival production subsidiary) and Muster Point Pictures (our high-end digital media, photography, drone cinematography, and visual storytelling subsidiary).

Our flagship creation, Piroake Fest, represents a masterclass in modern experiential design. It seamlessly integrates a curated picnic experience, a high-octane live karaoke arena, and a premium night rave into a unified showcase that attracts thousands of attendees, sponsors, food vendors, and local performers. By combining state-of-the-art spatial production with community-focused partnerships, we ensure that every single live event delivers maximum impact and memories that last forever.

Whether you are an attendee looking for a premium cultural experience, a local food merchant seeking high-revenue vendor opportunities, a sponsor aiming to connect directly with a highly engaged demographic, or a brand requiring cinematic video production from Muster Point Pictures, Walking-Fish Group is your ultimate collaborator. Explore our sub-brands, view our portfolio, or get in touch today to build something unforgettable.
