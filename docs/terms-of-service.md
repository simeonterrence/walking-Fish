## 5. Ticket Purchases & Terms

### 5.1 Ticket Types

The Services offer the following ticket types, each with specific terms:

- **Entry Passes:** One-time gate tickets scanned at venue entry. Types include Regular, VIP, and Group. Entry passes are non-rechargeable. Once scanned at the gate, they are marked as "used" and cannot be reused.
- **Parking Passes:** One-time parking tickets scanned at the parking lot entrance. Non-rechargeable and single-use.
- **Activity Credits:** Stored-value balance on a ticket that is debited per use at activity booths (games, karaoke, food, drinks, kids' zone). Activity credits are rechargeable via self-service top-up or at the venue's top-up booth.

### 5.2 Order Lifecycle

- **Unpaid:** An order has been created but payment has not been received. Tickets are not generated until payment is confirmed.
- **Pending Verification:** A Wave Transfer payment has been submitted and is awaiting administrative confirmation. Tickets are generated upon confirmation.
- **Paid:** Payment has been confirmed (via ModemPay webhook or admin verification). Tickets are generated and delivered by email.
- **Cancelled:** An order that has been cancelled before tickets were generated.
- **Refunded:** An order that has been cancelled after payment with a refund processed out-of-band.

### 5.3 Pricing & Currency

All prices are listed in Gambian Dalasi (GMD, "D"). Prices include all applicable taxes unless otherwise stated. We reserve the right to change prices at any time, but changes will not affect orders that have already been placed.

### 5.4 Capacity Limits

Each ticket type has a limited capacity. Orders are fulfilled on a first-come, first-served basis. If a ticket type sells out before your payment is confirmed, your order will be cancelled and you will not be charged (for ModemPay) or your payment will be refunded (for Wave Transfer, subject to Section 5.6).

### 5.5 Ticket Delivery

Upon payment confirmation, tickets are delivered by email to the email address provided at checkout to the address you provided. The email contains QR codes and ticket codes for each ticket. You may also access your tickets by logging into the ticket dashboard at `/tickets` using the magic link sent to your email.

It is your responsibility to ensure that the email address you provide is correct. We are not responsible for tickets delivered to an incorrect email address as a result of your input error.

### 5.6 Refunds & Cancellations

- **ModemPay Purchases:** All ticket sales are final. No refunds will be issued except in the case of event cancellation by Walking-Fish Group. If an event is cancelled, ticket holders will be notified and offered either a full refund or a credit toward a future event.
- **Wave Transfer Purchases:** Same as ModemPay — no refunds except for event cancellation.
- **Activity Credits:** Activity credit balances are non-refundable and non-transferable. Unused activity credits at the end of an event have no cash value and will not be refunded.
- **Event Postponement:** If an event is postponed (not cancelled), all tickets remain valid for the rescheduled date. No refunds will be issued for postponements.

### 5.7 Ticket Transfer

Tickets are non-transferable. You may not sell, assign, or otherwise transfer your ticket to another person. We reserve the right to invalidate any ticket that has been transferred in violation of these Terms.

## 6. Payment Terms

### 6.1 Payment Methods

We offer the following payment methods:

- **ModemPay (Primary):** An instant electronic payment gateway supporting Wave, QMoney, and AfriMoney mobile money. A 1.5% processor fee applies and is included in the displayed total. Payment is confirmed via webhook and tickets are delivered immediately upon confirmation.
- **Wave Transfer (Manual):** A manual transfer method with no platform fee. You send the exact amount to a designated Wave number and submit proof of payment (reference number and optional screenshot). Tickets are delivered upon administrative verification, typically within 2 hours during business hours.

### 6.2 Payment Confirmation

- **ModemPay:** Payment is confirmed automatically via webhook callback. If the webhook is delayed, tickets will be generated once the webhook is received. You may contact our team if payment has been taken but tickets are not received within 30 minutes.
