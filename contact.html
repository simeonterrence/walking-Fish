Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contact Walking-Fish Group for general inquiries?

Get in touch with Walking-Fish Group (WFG) to book an experience, explore sponsor packages, or discuss partnerships. General queries regarding our corporate structure, community events, or new partnership initiatives are routed to hello@walkingfish.gm.

What is the email address for vendor applications?

If you are interested in becoming a registered food, beverage, merchandise, or gaming vendor at our upcoming music festivals or rave events like Piroake Fest, direct applications and inquiries can be submitted to vendor@walkingfish.gm.

How do journalists request press credentials or an EPK?

For journalists, content creators, and broadcast stations seeking our official EPK (Electronic Press Kit), interviews, or festival press badges, our communications department can be reached directly at musterpoint@walkingfish.gm.

Where should I send production and media project inquiries?

If you are a brand manager or corporate executive looking to contract Muster Point Pictures for professional media production, drone cinematography, brand storytelling campaigns, or video editing, please contact our studio at musterpoint@walkingfish.gm.

About Walking-Fish Group & Our Commitment to Excellence

Walking-Fish Group stands at the forefront of creative entertainment and media production in West Africa. We are dedicated to delivering unforgettable experiences that transcend traditional boundaries. By blending innovative event management with high-end digital media production, we create a dynamic ecosystem that fosters culture, community, and connection. Our team is passionate about curating moments that resonate deeply with audiences, from massive music festivals to intimate gatherings.

We believe that every interaction should be meaningful. Whether through Walkie-Talkie Experiences, where we bring people together in physical spaces, or Muster Point Pictures, where we capture and share these stories digitally, our approach is always culture-first. We meticulously design each project to ensure that our creative vision aligns perfectly with the needs of our attendees, partners, and corporate sponsors. Our ultimate goal is to build an inclusive environment where art and technology thrive together.

Adaptability is our core philosophy, inspired by our namesake. We continuously evolve our strategies to stay ahead in a rapidly changing industry, ensuring that every event and production is not only relevant but extraordinary. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and a profound understanding of cultural dynamics, Walking-Fish Group continues to set new standards for excellence in entertainment. We invite you to join us on this journey of exploration and celebration, as we redefine what it means to connect in the modern world.
