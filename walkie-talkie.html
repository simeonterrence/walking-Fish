About

Live experiences that bring people together.

Walkie-Talkie Experiences is the live experiences and events arm of Walking-Fish Group. From festivals to intimate gatherings, we design moments that stay with people long after the music fades.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Walkie-Talkie Experiences?

Walkie-Talkie Experiences represents the vanguard of live entertainment and event design across The Gambia. As the dedicated events subsidiary of Walking-Fish Group, our primary mission is to establish physical spaces where music, connection, culture, and community naturally collide. We specialize in producing immersive experiences of all scales, turning standard gatherings into landmark cultural moments.

What types of events do you produce?

We produce a wide variety of live experiences. This ranges from high-energy acoustic showcase arenas and coastal rave festivals to curated public picnic pop-ups, custom karaoke arenas, and corporate brand activations. Whether it is an intimate indoor gathering or a large-scale outdoor festival, we handle the complete spatial layout and event production.

What makes a Walkie-Talkie Experience unique?

Every Walkie-Talkie event is built around a simple idea: the experience is the product. From the layout of the space to the sequence of the night, everything is meticulously designed for connection. We focus on detailed spatial layout, perfect vendor placement, premium sound engineering, and creative lighting schemes to craft unforgettable memories.

Can you tell me more about Piroake Fest?

Piroake Fest is our flagship creation and a masterclass in modern event integration. It seamlessly combines three distinct dynamics (a curated picnic area, a high-octane live karaoke arena, and a late-night rave experience) into a single seamless day of celebration. It brings together local artists, food vendors, and community members in an unparalleled cultural showcase.

How do you work with local vendors and artists?

We believe in community-focused event design. Through deep collaborations with Gambian food and beverage merchants, local artists, musicians, and corporate sponsors, we create events that reflect the energy and culture of the spaces they inhabit. We handle vendor partnership curation and ticketing to ensure a smooth, professional, and highly engaging workflow.

Our Experience Philosophy & Event Production

At Walkie-Talkie Experiences, we believe in a simple philosophy: we create events people don’t just attend, but remember.
