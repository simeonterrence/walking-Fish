More about Muster Point Pictures

Muster Point Pictures is the cutting-edge media, video production, and commercial storytelling subsidiary of Walking-Fish Group, headquartered in The Gambia, West Africa. Specializing in high-end video capture, event coverage, documentary filmmaking, corporate video projects, brand strategy, drone and aerial cinematography, and visual editing, we serve as the visual engine for regional and international brands. Our team utilizes state-of-the-art camera systems, stabilization gear, and professional drone equipment to produce cinematic visuals that carry an emotional impact and preserve critical creative moments for digital audiences.

From filming flagship music festivals, beach raves, and private acoustic shows organized by Walkie-Talkie Experiences to shooting promotional campaigns, television advertisements, and artist music videos, Muster Point Pictures works with a diverse roster of clients. We maintain complete control over the creative pipeline: from initial scriptwriting, spatial design, and storyboarding through on-set production and comprehensive post-production editing, color grading, and audio design. By combining technical excellence with a deep understanding of modern African culture and aesthetics, we deliver premium visual assets that tell unforgettable stories and elevate brand presence across all media channels.
